Katana Token Page

Introduction

Welcome to the Katana Token page! Our token is designed to be used for acquiring possessions in the cyber world. The token will always be available for 0.01 AVAX. In this world, corporations and governments are at war, and civilians are divided into factions based on robotics and cybernetic attachment modifications.

Minting

Our token can be minted by exchanging AVAX for Katana tokens. To mint tokens, please connect your wallet to the network and initiate a transaction for the desired amount of Katana tokens.

Purchase

Our token can be used to purchase possessions in the cyber world. Please browse our collection of items and use your Katana tokens to acquire them.

Lore

The cyber world is a place of constant conflict and change. Factions rise and fall, and new technologies emerge every day. The only constant is the need for power and resources. Our Katana token is the key to unlocking new possibilities and acquiring the tools needed to survive in this world.
